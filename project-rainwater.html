Smart Rainwater
Harvesting System

Mechanical Engineering · AutoCAD · Fluid Dynamics · Python

Overview

An innovative automated system that collects, filters, and intelligently manages rainwater for domestic use. The system addresses Sri Lanka's water scarcity challenges by providing a reliable, self-regulating water supply that reduces dependence on municipal infrastructure.

The system features a multi-stage filtration process, automated flow control valves, water level sensors, and a Python-based controller for optimal water management efficiency. Detailed fluid dynamics analysis was conducted using AutoCAD to ensure reliable performance under varying rainfall conditions and seasonal changes.

Key Highlights
  • Multi-stage filtration system for domestic-grade water quality
  • Automated flow control valves with real-time water level sensing
  • Python-based intelligent controller for optimal water management
  • Detailed fluid dynamics analysis for varying rainfall conditions
  • Full system design and schematics produced in AutoCAD
  • Scalable design adaptable to household and community-level use
