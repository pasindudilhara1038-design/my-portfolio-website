Note-to-Coin
Converter

Mechanical Engineering · SolidWorks · Motion Simulation

Overview

A mechanical system designed to accurately identify and exchange paper currency notes for their coin equivalents. The system uses optical sensors to detect and validate banknotes, a precision sorting mechanism to route them by denomination, and a secure coin dispensing unit calibrated for exact payouts.

Designed entirely in SolidWorks with full assembly modelling and motion simulation to validate mechanical performance before physical prototyping. Special attention was given to reliability under continuous operation and ease of maintenance.

Key Highlights
  • Optical banknote recognition using multi-point sensor arrays
  • Precision denomination-based mechanical sorting mechanism
  • Secure coin dispensing unit with exact payout calibration
  • Full 3D assembly and motion simulation in SolidWorks
  • Designed for high reliability under continuous operation
  • Modular design allows easy component replacement
